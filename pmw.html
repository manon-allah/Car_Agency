BMW_X1

Price :

1- In Egypt from 660,000 to 735,000.
2- In Saudi Arabia, 165,000 Saudi riyals.

Advantages :

1- More than one option for the engine.
2- Sharp handling makes driving fun.
3- Plenty of rear passenger space.
4- Good cargo space in the boot.

Disadvantages :

1- Low class safety.
2- Some cabin materials have hard plastic.
3- The ride quality gets a bit of a bend on the tough off-road.
4- The interior is fairly simple.

BMW_X2

Price :

1- In Egypt from 650,000 to 700,000 EGP.
2- In Saudi Arabia 195,000 Saudi riyals.

Advantages :

1- The material quality and build quality are first class.
2- Fast acceleration and sports handling.
3- Intuitive technology features that are easy to use.

Disadvantages :

1- Rear seat space and load space less than x1.
2- The price is above average for the four-wheel drive class.
3- Ride quality is excessively harsh for a luxury car.
4- The weak brakes make it difficult to stop smoothly in traffic.

BMW_X3

Price :

1- In Egypt from 1,325,000 to 1,475,000 EGP.
2- In Saudi Arabia from 226,000 to 322,000 Saudi riyals.

Advantages :

1- A variety of standard and optional features.
2- Ample interior space for both front and rear seat passengers.
3- Multiple powerful drive options.
4- An easy-to-use infotainment system.
5- Plenty of passenger and cargo space.

Disadvantages :

1- More expensive than most of the competitors.
2- For an interior less elegant than some competitors.
3- Android Auto is clearly absent from the options menu.
